PG Complaint Management System

A centralized platform for PG residents to report issues such as maintenance, electricity, water supply, or cleanliness while enabling administrators to efficiently track, manage, and resolve complaints.

System Features

Easy Complaint Submission

Residents can quickly submit complaints related to maintenance, water supply, electricity, or other PG facilities.

Real-Time Complaint Tracking

Students can track the status of their complaints and receive updates as the issue progresses.

Powerful Admin Dashboard

Administrators can review complaints, update status, assign resolutions, and monitor all reported issues.
